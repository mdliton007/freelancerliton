How to Choose an Email Marketing Platform

Email marketing is one of the fastest growing trends on the planet, and it's no surprise. Last year, the number of email users clocked in at 3.9 billion, and that number is projected to rise to 4.3 billion within a couple of years. Anyone can see email as the marketing goldmine that it is, but it takes a smart marketer to take advantage of this opportunity to reach their target audience, leads, and customers faster, more efficiently, and more effectively than ever before.

The trouble is that when it comes to email marketing software, there are enough choices to keep your head spinning indefinitely. And there is a boatload of features that you can get from each of the various services. Regardless of the size of your business, which industry, and who your target audience is, there are a few features that you just can't (or shouldn't) do without. Check out these 3 necessary email marketing features.

Email Marketing Software Pricing: What to Expect

Like all things, email marketing platforms come in a range of pricing options. There are basic plans and premium ones that will come with a wider range of features and services. You can find a basic plan from Constant Contact that lets you send unlimited emails and provides basic Ecommerce tools for just $20 a month. Meanwhile, a premium plan from SendinBlue will cost you $66 a month, and prices can climb even higher.

You can go really big and splurge for the Max plan from GetResponse. That'll cost you $165/month and includes multiple users, campaign consultants, and great reporting tools. Or you can opt for a company like Fiverr to get email marketing services starting at just $5 a pop.

Email Marketing Software: The Game Changer You've Been Waiting For

It really just depends on your budget and what you want to get out of your email campaign service. The best rule of thumb is to find a platform that has a decent amount of features to fit your needs while still working within the price range you can comfortably afford. Email marketing is a big priority, and marketing experts the world over will tell you that a good platform is worth investing in. So bear that in mind when making your calculations.
